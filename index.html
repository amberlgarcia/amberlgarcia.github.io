About

Operating at the intersection of people, strategy & execution

I build the operating foundation that lets ambitious organizations execute with clarity. My work sits at the intersection of strategy, systems, and people — most often in organizations where complexity, growth, or change have started to slow execution. I bring structure without bureaucracy, strategic partnership without politics, and the kind of organizational clarity that turns a capable team into one that executes.

Particularly effective as a strategic partner to executive leaders navigating growth, transformation, or reinvention — translating strategy into execution, aligning the right people around the real constraint, and staying close enough to the work to ensure momentum sticks.

A decade at Experian has meant building operating systems from scratch, resetting how a 1,500-person organization plans and prioritizes, scaling an eight-figure marketplace business from proof of concept, and leading one of the company's highest-profile product launches. I've earned the Experian "Elite" Award (Top 2% globally) for impact that goes beyond the role.
